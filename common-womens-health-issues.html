Understanding Common Women's Health Issues

Menstrual Disorders: Navigating Irregularities Menstrual irregularities can cause physical discomfort and emotional distress. Our gynaecology experts can help diagnose and treat conditions such as heavy or prolonged periods, painful menstruation, and irregular cycles, providing relief and improving your quality of life.

Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS): Managing Hormonal Imbalances

PCOS is a common hormonal disorder affecting women of reproductive age. Our gynaecologists specialize in diagnosing and managing PCOS, helping you navigate its symptoms, such as irregular periods, weight gain, and fertility issues, through personalized treatment plans.

Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs): Addressing Uncomfortable Symptoms

UTIs can cause discomfort, pain, and frequent urination. Our gynaecology department offers accurate diagnosis and effective treatment options to alleviate UTI symptoms, reduce the risk of recurrence, and promote overall urinary health.

Endometriosis: Alleviating Pain and Improving Quality of Life

Endometriosis is a condition where the tissue lining the uterus grows outside of it, causing pain and infertility. Our gynaecology specialists have extensive experience in diagnosing and managing endometriosis. We offer a range of treatment options, including medication, hormonal therapy, and minimally invasive surgical procedures, to alleviate pain and improve your quality of life.

Fibroids: Treating Benign Uterine Growths

Fibroids are noncancerous growths that develop in the uterus, leading to symptoms like heavy menstrual bleeding, pelvic pain, and pressure. At Ravina Hospital, our gynaecologists employ various treatment approaches, such as medication, non-invasive procedures, and surgical interventions, to address fibroids and relieve associated symptoms.

Menopause: Embracing the Transition with Grace

Menopause marks the end of a woman's reproductive years and comes with various physical and emotional changes. Our gynaecologists understand the challenges of menopause and provide comprehensive care to manage symptoms like hot flashes, mood swings, and bone health issues. We offer hormone replacement therapy, lifestyle counselling, and alternative treatments to help you embrace this transition with grace.
